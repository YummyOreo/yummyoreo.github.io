About Me

Hi! I'm a high school student from the Chicagoland area that loves to play ultimate frisbee, take pictures (you can find some of them in my portfolio), make websites, and write-especially on the history of medicine.


I currently work with TBFighters-an advocacy group pushing against TB, the world's deadliest infectious disease-through policy and pressure campaigns. I am one of their lead maintainers for their website.


Throughout Chicago, I try to document life here through my photojournalism.
